In Croatian, "moreška" and all its declensions (moreške, morešku, moreškom, moreški) are **lowercase** — it is a common noun (a type of dance), not a proper name.

**Why:** Confirmed by Josip in session on 2026-05-20. Croatian orthography treats it as a common noun.

**How to apply:** When writing or editing HR translations, always use lowercase for moreška and its forms. Uppercase only at the literal start of a sentence (standard grammar rule). In English translations, keep "Moreška" capitalised as a proper name.
