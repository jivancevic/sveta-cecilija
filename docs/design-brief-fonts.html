Layout & character

Cinematic, then quiet.

The homepage is dark and cinematic — a full-bleed hero video over black, with a silver-foil gradient on the brand name. Inner pages exhale onto warm parchment, keeping the same Bodoni headlines and gold accents for continuity.

Notes for the designer

  • Plain <img> everywhere — no next/image yet, so high-quality source files are enough; multiple sizes aren't needed.
  • "moreška" vs "Moreška" — lowercase in Croatian (common noun), capitalised in English (proper noun). Important for any new copy or mockups.
  • No em-dashes in user-visible copy — use commas, colons, or periods. Em-dashes read as "AI-written" in this voice.
  • Hero animation — overlays fade in at 0 s, logo at 0.3 s, name rises at 0.6 s, "est." line at 1.0 s. Posters are extracted at 0.5 s so first paint is never black.
  • Accent rule — every eyebrow is followed by a 36 px × 1 px gold hairline. It's the single recurring decorative motif across the site.
