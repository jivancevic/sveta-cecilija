# Door-staff exit script — review cards (GitHub #43)

Brief **every** door-staff member on this at the start of the season. Handing
the card is part of the standard exit procedure, the same as saying goodnight.

## The one line (say it, then hand the card)

> **EN —** "If you enjoyed the show, please scan this to leave us a quick review. Thank you, and goodnight!"
>
> **HR —** "Ako vam se predstava svidjela, molimo skenirajte ovo i ostavite kratku recenziju. Hvala vam i laku noć!"

Hand one card to each guest/group as they leave. Smile, brief eye contact, then
move to the next guest — don't slow the exit flow.

## Rules

- **Never offer anything in return** for a review (no discount, free drink,
  next-ticket perk — nothing). It violates Google's and Tripadvisor's terms and
  can get the listing penalised. The ask is the favour; that's all.
- Ask **everyone**, not only people who look happy — don't cherry-pick for
  positive reviews (also a TOS issue).
- It's a request, never pressure. If someone declines or walks past, let it go.
- The card has both Tripadvisor (front) and Google (back) — guests pick whichever
  they use. Either is great.

## If a guest asks "how?"

> "Just point your phone camera at the square code — it opens the review form.
> Takes about 30 seconds."

## Restock

Keep a small stack at the exit. Flag the box to refill before it runs low so
no performance ends without cards on hand.
